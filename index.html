• Are Fortisocks like normal socks?

    No, Fortisocks are not like any other sock on the market. They are so thin, they are "see through" when wearing them.

  • Are they like Trusox?

    No, Fortisocks are nothing like Trusox. Trusox are a stand alone sports sock, which among other things, improve grip between your sports sock and football cleat. In contrast, Fortisocks are worn beneath your sports socks and act as a second skin, reducing the formation of blisters.

  • How long will they last?

    The life of Fortisocks is dependent on a wide variety of factors including training intensity, boot tightness and physiological idiosyncrasies of the athlete. Further an altered gait during running caused by existing blisters or other injuries may also affect the life of Fortisocks.

    As Fortisocks are ultra-thin, they do not have a prolonged life, however this trait is essential to their blister prevention capabilities and is a contributing factor which makes Fortisocks so comfortable. For example, Australian National hockey team players and Commonwealth Gold medallists, Rachael Lynch and Ashleigh Nelson trekked the treacherous Kokoda Jungle for nine days wearing Fortisocks. To the amazement of their guides as well as fellow trekkers, they did not experience blisters even though they we wearing brand new shoes. Further they did not even require one full pack for this period.

    In many cases one pair of Fortisocks have lasted up to one months of trainings / games. However with different athletes, under different conditions, participating in a different activity, Fortisocks may have only lasted one or two trainings before having to change pairs. Therefore their life is dependent on numerous, co-dependent factors including the type of athlete who is using them and their activity.

    Taking this into consideration we enclosed 3 pairs of Fortisocks per pack, to ensure that every athlete should be able to at least “break in” their new boots or runners with one pack.

  • Do I treat these like normal socks when wearing and washing?

    Yes, however when placing on your feet follow the instructions enclosed in the package to prolong their life. The washing instructions are seen on the product package, however please remember it is recommended to always wash before use. Like all types of apparel items, residue or colour from the manufacturing process remains on the socks after production. To ensure colour does not run from the product while in use, please remember to wash before use.

  • Are they really "One Size Fits All"?

    Due to Fortisocks ultra thin composition, they are able to contour to almost every foot size. The the only difference being the height to which they reach on your leg. For example for a size 11 mens, Fortisocks would be low crew sock, whereas for a size 11 women's it would be a mid crew sock.

  • What is their exact size specifications?

    Fortisocks are 28.5cm in length and 8.5cm in width. However they can stretch up to 50cm in length and 17.5cm in width without breaking!!

  • Can my children wear them?

    Of course they can! Fortisocks can easily be folded if they are too high up the leg.

  • Can Fortisocks be used as stand alone sock?

    Fortisocks were specifically designed to be used as an inner sock, worn underneath convention sports socks. It is not recommended to use them as a stand alone sock as this will severely diminish their life and effectiveness of their function.
