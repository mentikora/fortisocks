    3 Specially designed to be used during intense exercise where the foot is commonly placed under high friction forces. The hydrophobic properties of these materials reduce moisture build-up on the foot as a result of their ability to transfer sweat from the surface of the foot to the outer sock. In reducing moisture build up on the foot, the skin in less susceptible to blister formation, as it is well known that excess moisture softens skin, increasing the risk of blistering.
